ASSUMPTIONS MADE FOR THE APPLICATION OF MOSS ET AL. (2006) EQUATIONS
by Emily Mongold
8 June 2022

These assumptions were made from a combination of correspondence with Robb Moss, use of the Cliq software, and correspondence with the Cliq team. 

1. the I_C soil behavior type parameter should be calculated, and any layer with I_C > 2.6 is considered not liquefiable.

2. any layer above the water table is considered not liquefiable
